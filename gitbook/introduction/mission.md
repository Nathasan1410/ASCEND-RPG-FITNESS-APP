## Why ASCEND?

**Problem:** Traditional fitness apps are repetitive and boring. Users lose motivation after a few weeks.

**Solution:** Gamify fitness with RPG mechanics. Every workout is a quest. Every milestone is a level up. Progress becomes tangible and rewarding.
