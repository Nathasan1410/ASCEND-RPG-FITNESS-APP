# Quick Start

Get started with ASCEND in 5 minutes.

## What You'll Need

| Requirement | Details |
| ----------- | ------- |
| Device | Desktop or mobile browser |
| Account | Create or use demo account |
| Time | 15-90 minutes for first quest |

## Step 1: Access the App

1. Go to [ascend-fitness.vercel.app](https://ascend-fitness.vercel.app)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Login"**
3. Create account or use demo credentials

## Step 3: Generate Your First Quest

1. Go to **"Quests"** page
2. Click **"Generate New Quest"**
3. Select your preferences:
   - Duration (15-90 min)
   - Equipment type
   - Goals (optional)
4. Click **"Generate"**

## Step 4: Complete the Quest

1. Review your quest details
2. Follow exercise instructions
3. Complete all sets and reps
4. Note your completion time

## Step 5: Upload Proof

1. After completing, click **"Upload Proof"**
2. Upload photo or video
3. Add any notes
4. Click **"Submit"**

## Step 6: Get Evaluated

1. OPIK AI evaluates your proof
2. Receive score (S-E grade)
3. XP awarded based on score
4. See your new total XP
