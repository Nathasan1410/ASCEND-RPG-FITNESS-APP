## Your First Quest Walkthrough

### Step 1: Login
1. Go to `/login`
2. Enter demo credentials (or sign up)
3. Access your dashboard

### Step 2: Generate Quest
1. Click "Generate Quest" button
2. Select your equipment
3. Choose your goals
4. Wait for AI to create your personalized quest

### Step 3: Complete Quest
1. Read through exercises
2. Follow warm-up routine
3. Complete each exercise with proper form
4. Track your sets and reps
5. Finish with cool-down

### Step 4: Upload Proof
1. Click "Complete Quest"
2. Upload photo/video proof
3. Add notes about your performance
4. Submit for evaluation

### Step 5: Earn XP
1. OPIK AI evaluates your completion
2. Receive XP based on:
   - Quest difficulty
   - Form quality (40%)
   - Effort level (30%)
   - Consistency (30%)
3. Check your stats dashboard!

## Hunter Ranking System

ASCEND uses a 6-tier rank system from E to S. Each rank unlocks new features and challenges.

### Rank Progression

| Rank | Level Range | XP Range | Features |
|------|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| **E-Rank** | 1-9 | 0-1,500 | Basic bodyweight quests |
| **D-Rank** | 10-19 | 1,500-5,000 | Equipment-based workouts |
| **C-Rank** | 20-29 | 5,000-12,000 | Intermediate exercises |
| **B-Rank** | 30-39 | 12,000-25,000 | Advanced multi-exercise |
| **A-Rank** | 40-49 | 25,000-45,000 | Complex high-intensity |
| **S-Rank** | 50+ | 45,000+ | Elite performance quests |

### Rank Benefits

- **Higher XP Rewards**: S-Rank quests earn up to 4,000 XP
- **Better Equipment Access**: Unlocks advanced workout equipment
- **Social Features**: Full access to Hunter Network feed
- **Leaderboard Visibility**: Compete on global rankings
- **Priority AI Evaluation**: Faster quest generation
