## Step 1: Generate Your First Quest

1. **Navigate to Dashboard**
   - Click "Dashboard" in the navigation menu
   - You'll see your current stats and active quest area

2. **Click "Generate Quest"**
   - Look for the prominent "Generate Quest" button
   - It's usually near the center of the dashboard

3. **Select Your Preferences**
   The app will ask for your preferences:
   
   - **Available Equipment**: Check what you have
     - [ ] Dumbbells
     - [ ] Barbell
     - [ ] Kettlebells
     - [ ] Resistance Bands
     - [ ] Bodyweight only
   
   - **Fitness Goals**: Choose your primary focus
     - [ ] Strength
     - [ ] Hypertrophy
     - [ ] Endurance
     - [ ] Weight Loss
   
   - **Quest Difficulty**: Choose difficulty
     - [ ] Easy (recommended for first quest)
     - [ ] Moderate
     - [ ] Hard

4. **Wait for AI Generation**
   - Groq AI will generate your personalized quest (1-3 seconds)
   - A notification will show when ready

5. **Review Your Quest**
   Check the quest details:
   - **Title**: Quest name (e.g., "Foundation Strength Protocol")
   - **Description**: Brief overview of the workout
   - **Difficulty**: E, D, C, B, A, or S
   - **Estimated Time**: How long the workout should take
   - **XP Reward**: Base XP you'll earn for completion

---

## Step 2: Prepare for Your Workout

1. **Find a Space**
   - Clear area for exercises
   - Ensure good ventilation
   - Have water nearby
   - Keep phone or timer accessible

2. **Check Equipment**
   - Verify you have all equipment listed in quest
   - Test weights are manageable
   - Ensure enough space for movements

3. **Preview Exercises**
   Before starting:
   - Read through all exercises in your quest
   - Watch demonstration videos (if unfamiliar with exercises)
   - Note down sets, reps, and weights

---

## Step 3: Complete the Warm-up

1. **Follow Warm-up Instructions**
   - Your quest starts with a warm-up section
   - Complete all warm-up exercises as specified
   - Don't skip this - it prevents injuries

2. **Warm-up Duration**
   - Typically 5-10 minutes
   - Includes light cardio and dynamic stretching
   - Prepares muscles for main workout

---

## Step 4: Execute Main Exercises

1. **Track Each Exercise**
   - Mark sets as you complete them
   - Note actual reps vs. target reps
   - Record weights used

2. **Follow Rest Periods**
   - Each exercise has a recommended rest time
   - Use built-in timer or your phone
   - Rest is crucial for quality performance

3. **Listen to Your Body**
   - If something feels wrong, stop
   - Reduce weight if form breaks down
   - Quality > quantity

4. **Stay Hydrated**
   - Drink water between exercises
   - Don't wait until you're thirsty

---

## Step 5: Complete the Cool-down

1. **Don't Skip Cool-down**
   - Follow all cool-down exercises
   - Static stretches hold for 15-30 seconds each
   - Helps reduce muscle soreness
   - Lowers heart rate gradually

2. **Cool-down Duration**
   - Typically 5-10 minutes
   - Light cardio followed by static stretching

---

## Step 6: Upload Proof

**Proof is REQUIRED to earn XP**

1. **Choose Proof Type**
   - **Photo**: Take a picture after workout
   - **Video**: Record your completion (better for verification)

2. **Capture Requirements**
   - Include your face or the equipment in the frame
   - Show the completed quest summary
   - Ensure good lighting
   - File must be under 10MB

3. **Upload Process**
   - Click "Complete Quest" after finishing exercises
   - Select or capture photo/video
   - Wait for upload to complete
   - Enter any notes about your completion

4. **Actual Duration**
   - Enter how long the workout actually took (in minutes)
   - This is used by the AI judge for effort scoring

## Tips for First-Time Hunters

### Before Your First Quest
- **Start Easy**: Choose E or D difficulty for first quest
- **Focus on Form**: Quality over quantity
- **Rest Adequately**: Don't rush between sets
- **Stay Hydrated**: Drink water throughout

### During Your First Quest
- **Listen to Body**: Stop if something feels wrong
- **Don't Compare**: Everyone starts somewhere
- **Take Breaks**: If needed, rest between exercises
- **Celebrate Small Wins**: Completing each set is progress

### After Your First Quest
- **Record Everything**: Note weights, reps, and how you felt
- **Rest and Recover**: Don't do the same workout next day
- **Reflect on What Went Well**: Repeat what worked
- **Identify Areas to Improve**: Plan to work on weaknesses

---

## Common First-Time Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Skipping Warm-up/Cool-down**
   - Always complete these sections
   - They prevent injuries and improve recovery

2. **Starting Too Heavy**
   - Start with lighter weights
   - Focus on proper form
   - Increase weight gradually

3. **Rushing Through Exercises**
   - Quality reps are more important than speed
   - Take your time with each movement

4. **Not Tracking Properly**
   - Mark sets as you complete them
   - Use the built-in timer for rest periods

5. **Forgetting to Upload Proof**
   - Proof is REQUIRED for XP
   - Upload immediately after completing the quest

6. **Ignoring AI Judge Feedback**
   - Read the feedback carefully
   - Implement suggested improvements
   - This helps you level up faster
